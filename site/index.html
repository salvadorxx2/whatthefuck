SUPPLIES

  • CKN tenders
  • CAPT Pauls REDFISH MAJIC
  • Garlic
  • S&P
  • BLCK beans
  • Rice
  • Butter
  • Tinfoil
  • George Foreman
  • Tortillas

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Remove tendons from ckn
  2. Cover ckn in redfish majic
  3. Lightly coat tinfoil w butter
  4. Place ckn in tinfoil, wrap it all up in nice packet
  5. Preheat George
  6. Mince garlic, sautee until fragrant
  7. S&P garlic, add beans with majority liquid
  8. Simmer beans, season as needed
  9. George should b hot, cook ckn until 165 degrees
  10. Shred cooked ckn
  11. Cook rice
  12. Plate, add garnish as needed
  13. Enjoy
